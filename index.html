About Me

I’m Leon Sacco, a Multimedia Software Development student with a strong foundation in both creative and technical disciplines. With experience spanning front-end design, back-end development, data reporting, and IT security collaboration, I enjoy crafting digital solutions that are both user-friendly and technically robust.

My journey has included building accessible web applications, generating dynamic reports with Power BI, conducting access reviews, and supporting IT Security teams on internal campaigns. I’ve also worked on visual design projects, leveraging tools like Adobe Creative Suite and GIMP to create engaging, informative content that aligns with business goals.

I thrive in cross-functional environments where I can combine my coding skills in Python, C#, and HTML/CSS with a designer’s eye for clarity and usability. Whether it’s prototyping a personalised health interface using AI or automating data processes, I’m always looking to bridge the gap between form and function.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about music curation, tech trends, and constantly exploring new tools and frameworks. I bring a growth mindset, attention to detail, and a user-first approach to everything I do.
